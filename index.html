Eazyfilter


Our idea


Do you ever have that problem where you are looking for that one piece of clothing but everytime you go onto a website you have to change the clothing filter? Well here is your solution Eazyfilter is a google chrome add-on that you can use to preset filters so you don't have to make a new clothing filter evrytime you go on a new website! :)

How do you use Eazyfilter?


-you download our google chrome add-on (coming soon)
- you make the clothing filter to fit what you are searching (price, colour, type of clothing)
-go onto the website where youu want to find your item (farfetch, harrods etc..)
-our add-on automaticly searches for the item if it is available
- put your item in the cart and order it. simple as that.
